AHS Hacks Moor is a free 5 hour hackathon aimed at students from Alhambra High School. No previous coding experience is required!


We want to ensure that all students are able to explore and mature a desire in the field of computer science by providing opportunities for software and career development

Frequently Asked Questions


What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a timed coding competition where students come together to build something using technology!

How much does it cost?

Nothing! Attending is completely free

Where is this event?

AHS Hacks Moor is being hosted at Alhambra High School

Will there be prizes?

Yes! Check back at a later date to see the full list of prizes

What if I don't know how to code?

Don't worry! Anyone can attend even if they don't know anything. AHS Hacks Moor is meant for you to gain experience and have fun working with others

Is there a theme?

A theme will be announced at the opening ceremony

Is this an individual event?

Nope! Bring your friends! You can have up to 4 people in a group. If you don't have a group and want to be in one, we'll help you find one

What if my question isn't listed on here?

You can email us at info@alhambrahacksmoor.org
